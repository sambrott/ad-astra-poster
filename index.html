Master Class

Tobias Van Schneider is my design North Star. A specialist in web design, he creates elegant work across mediums in digital, print and industrial. In this movie poster for Ad Astra, Van Schneider uses type as the key element to emphasize the lack of connection between the protagonist while alone in space searching for his father.

Found Asset

Using a javascript animation by Antoine Wodniack found on Codepen, I sought to recreate the wormhole effect with the typography used in the original composition.

Transform using AI

Using Cursor’s AI agent Composer 2, I transformed the original wormhole into one composed of the “LOSS OF SIGNAL” text. I also included an animation of the astronaut descending into the wormhole.

The same canvas pipeline drives both the CodePen-inspired wormhole and the final poster: discs, particles, and layered typography in IBM Plex Mono. Use the controls to explore type scale, motion speed, hole size, astronaut visibility, and vertical position.

Physical Manipulation

Adding a torn piece of paper to the bottom section of the poster directs attention away from the title and towards the movement on the upper third. The torn paper disappears as the astronaut reaches the bottom to reveal the rest of the copy.
